International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.

  • Delivery typically takes 7-14 business days
  • All duties, taxes, and customs fees are the responsibility of the customer
  • Tracking information provided for all international orders

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2pm EST Monday-Friday are processed the same day. Orders placed after 2pm or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.

Return Policy

30-Day Returns

We want you to love your Auvier purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, you may return eligible items within 30 days of delivery.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • All original tags must be attached
  • Items must be returned in original packaging
  • Undergarments, swimwear, and items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned

How to Return

  1. Log into your account and navigate to your order history
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return and reason for return
  3. Print the prepaid return shipping label
  4. Pack items securely in original packaging
  5. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds are issued to your original payment method. Please allow an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

Note: Original shipping charges are non-refundable. Return shipping is complimentary for domestic orders within the US.

Exchanges

For the fastest service, we recommend returning your item for a refund and placing a new order for your desired size or color. This ensures your new item is reserved and ships immediately.

If you need assistance with an exchange, please contact our customer service team at support@auvier.com.

Need Assistance?
